About Me

Whether it's a data pipeline, a dashboard, or a horror story narrated by AI – I’m all about bringing ideas to life through tech.

My expertise lies in Python scripting, web and data analytics, cloud infrastructure (Google Cloud, AWS), and API integrations. I enjoy bridging the gap between technical complexity and human experience. Whether I’m building dashboards for data teams or producing creepy content that triggers goosebumps, I always aim to leave an impact. I believe in using technology not just to automate and analyze – but to inspire, engage, and even entertain.

If you're into building cool things, solving hard problems, or exploring how AI can tell better stories – we're already on the same wavelength. Let's connect, collaborate, and create something awesome (or scary).
